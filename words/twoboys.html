Two Boys


This twelve-page long poem tells the story of a teenage boy and his first sexual encounter. Dreamlike in style and accompanied by eight free-handed illustrations, the book reccounts a point of no return in his life, an experience after which people around him can't be looked at the same way, and proposes an exploration of sexuality that questions the morality of intimacy between two individuals of different age.
